# Risk Control

## Goal

Keep the script usable under Douyin-style traffic and review pressure while protecting follow growth.

## Hard Fail Items

Any one of these means immediate fail:

- direct forced follow wording
- fake certainty like “100% 爆”
- fake platform guarantee like “保证上热门”
- hard money promise like “肯定月入多少”
- direct bait like “评论区扣 XX” or “我直接给你 XX”
- AI phrasing that makes machine output pretend to be pure human original work

## Medium Risk Items

These do not auto-fail, but should be rewritten if they weaken stability:

- too many abstract words in the second half
- too much theory after the steps
- two or more CTAs in one ending
- no proof anchor
- conditional preview instead of statement preview

## AI Expression Boundary

Allowed:

- AI 辅助写稿
- AI 辅助提效
- AI 辅助剪辑

Avoid:

- AI 直接帮你做完
- AI 自动帮你搞定
- AI 替你决定内容方向

## Closing Safety

Safest ending type:

- statement-style next clue
- save CTA

Example:

`后面我接着拆它怎么落地。这条你先存着。`

## Risk Review Output

The risk reviewer must return:

- pass or fail
- exact risky sentence
- risk type
- safer replacement line
